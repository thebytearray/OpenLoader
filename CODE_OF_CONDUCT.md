# Code of conduct

We want collaboration on OpenLoader to stay respectful and constructive. By participating, you agree to uphold a baseline of courtesy toward other contributors and users.

For a detailed standard, many communities adopt the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/). Project maintainers may remove or edit abusive content and temporarily or permanently exclude anyone who harasses, trolls, or otherwise disrupts the project.

Report concerns to the maintainers via the contact points listed on [thebytearray.org](https://thebytearray.org) or through GitHub’s reporting tools for issues in this repository.
